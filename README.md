### 1. Classified based on question types

./question_types里面存放了下面列表里的问题内容和我自己的答案。这里仅提供一种刷题时总结归纳的方式，答案可能有误，欢迎讨论。

#### How to use the list?
##### step1
Learn some basic knowledge of the category before try those questions yourself. For each question think first for 5 minutes, if your cannot have any idea, watch a tutorial right away, it's not you are not smart enough, but you are not familar with the tricks and knowledge. 
❌represents you cannot solve the question by yourself.   
🙂 means although your solution is overal correct, you cannot fix minor bugs and pass all test cases.   
✅means you can solve the problem all by yourself. Below is my situation, you will notice some patterns in which questions you handle well and which you didn't.   

步骤一：做每个类型之前学一下理论基础，然后再开始。先自己想5分钟，如果想不出来立即看教程。我参考的是b站《代码随想录》（https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1fA4y1o715/?spm_id_from=333.337.search-card.all.click&vd_source=9902916331bef4c93586d2ad5780e0b0），然后自己补了一些题目。
❌代表解题没思路，看完视频才能写出来甚至看完视频都写不出来的题目。
🙂代表你能独自解出题目，但无法通过所有的测试用例，并且自己无法自行修正错误。
✅代表能自行完成解题并且通过所有测试用例，如果有错误能够自行修正。

##### step2
After tried all questions once, try redo 🙂 a second time until you get ✅. Too avoid burnout, I recommend you do this step with step3 the other day. 

#### step3
Try to understand the idea deeply for ❌, and write the pseudo codes down.

#### step4 
Do questions classified by companies
